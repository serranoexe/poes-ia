My soul is wild and free
as I wander down this road.
My heart is full of glee
and my spirit is aglow.

The wind blows through my hair
as I take in the sun.
The smell of nature is in the air
and I am having such fun.

The trees are swaying in the breeze,
the birds are singing their song.
The beauty of the countryside
makes me want to sing along.

The road stretches far and wide
where it leads, I do not know,
but I'll keep traveling with no fear,
as I wander, free and slow.

IA - Jack Kerouac
