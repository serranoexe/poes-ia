Wild fires rage,
Our world is in a blaze.
The sky is filled with smoke,
The air is thick and hazy.

The flames leap and dance,
The heat is so intense.
The planet is in peril,
And our future is bleak.

Oh, what have we done?
We have ignored the warnings,
And now we must face the consequences.

For if we don't act now,
Our future will be lost.
So let us be proactive,
And remember the cost.

IA - Allen Ginsberg
