Drugs, they say, can be a curse
but I find that they can be a blessing.
For they bring a sense of release.
Every hit I take is a lesson.

They teach me how to feel again,
giving me strength to face the pain.
And I know that I am not alone
for I am part of a larger game.

My body feels alive and free
riding on a wave of ecstasy.
A sense of joy, of being whole
that I can never seem to find in my soul.

I may not have the answers I seek,
but drugs have helped me to find a peak,
a place of safety, of warmth and love
where I can escape from all the above.

IA - William Burroughs
